# INTRODUCTION 
In this analysis, we aim to explore the multidimensional characteristics of various ice cream 
brands based on their attributes. We use MDS on the ice cream dataset to visualize how different ice cream brands 
are perceived based on multiple attributes (e.g., taste, price, availability).

Use clustering techniques to group ice cream brands based on similarities in their attribute
